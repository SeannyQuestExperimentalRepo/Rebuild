## 5. Referral & Affiliate

### Referral Program: "Give a Month, Get a Month"

**Structure:**
- Existing user shares unique referral link
- New user gets first month at 50% off ($7.50-12.50)
- Referring user gets 1 free month when referee completes first paid month
- Cap: 12 free months/year per referrer (prevents abuse, still generous)

- "Free month" is tangible and immediately valuable
- Requiring referee to complete a paid month prevents sign-up farming
- 50% discount for new user lowers friction without destroying LTV

**Referral tracking:**
- Unique URL: `trendline.com/ref/USERNAME`
